I loved the book Harry Potter (JK Rowling)


The Harry Potter books are comforting stories that guide us through some of the most challenging moments in our lives. The writing is beautiful, the characters are memorable, and there are enough hidden details in the series to keep us entertained for years to come.

Harry James Potter is a fictional character and the titular protagonist in J. K. Rowling's series of eponymous novels. 

Lord Voldemort is a sobriquet for Tom Marvolo Riddle, a fictional character and the main antagonist in J. K. Rowling's series of Harry Potter novels.
